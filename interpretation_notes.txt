Interpretation Guide:
---------------------
Î” Pass Defense Percentile (delta_pass_def):
    Positive values mean a team improved its pass defense ranking from last year.

Mixed-Effects Regression:
    Tests whether double-drafting teams improve more than single/zero drafters.

player_contribution:
    Measures how much a DB (weighted by snap share) contributed to the team's overall improvement.

Outputs:
---------
- team_improvement_regression.csv        : Regression coefficients (effect size by group)
- bootstrapped_team_improvement.csv      : 95% confidence intervals for group means
- player_contribution_summary.csv        : Average player-level contributions by draft group
- team_improvement_by_group.png          : Violin + jitter plot for team improvement
- player_contribution_scatter.png        : Scatter of snap share vs contribution
